## Summary

Antpool (operated by Bitmain) officially launched HNS mining support in December 2022 alongside Bitmain's release of the Antminer HS3 — the first high-performance ASIC miner purpose-built for the Handshake algorithm. Their entry was significant: within 24 hours of launch, Antpool captured approximately 24% of total HNS network hashrate, pushing F2Pool below 50% share for the first time. Antpool launched with a 0% promotional fee and has maintained HNS mining support since.

## Notable Strengths

- **Launched during a bear market** — Antpool entered HNS mining in December 2022, one of the lowest price periods for HNS. This is the opposite of fair-weather behavior.
- **Backed by Bitmain** — As the manufacturer of HNS ASICs (Antminer HS3), Bitmain and Antpool have a direct financial interest in HNS network health.
- **Rapid hashrate contribution** — Antpool became a major hashrate contributor almost immediately after launch.
- **Multi-language support** — Pool interface available in multiple languages.

---

## Areas to Watch

- As a large multi-coin pool, Antpool could theoretically deprioritize HNS if profitability drops severely.
- No documented bear market exit as of last update, but the pool is relatively new to HNS (launched late 2022).
